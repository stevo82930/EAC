Jesus, the Baptism

Preached by Elder Stephen Rees

Analysis of the life of Christ (For Context)

  • Introduction (mt 1)
  • Infancy (mt 2)
    • John the Baptist born
  • Year of Preparation (mt 3 (Jn 1:19-4; lk 3-4:30; mk 1:1-13))
    • John the Baptist baptizing art the river Jordan
  • Year of Popularity (mt 4:12)
    • John the Baptist arrested
  • Year of Passion (mt 14 -27 (jn 6; lk 9-23; mk 10-15))
    • John the Baptist beheaded
  • The Risen Life (mt 28-Acts)


Verses we will explore today:

  • John 1:19-34 (Year of Preparation, John preaching)
    • This is the main passage. These other passages will be used to expound on this.
  • Matthew 3 (Year of Preparation, John preaching)
  • Matthew 11 (Year of Popularity, John in prison)
  • Matthew 17 (Year of Passion, John is dead)
  • These verses outside of the Gospels will be used:
    • Micah 3,4; Isaiah 11; Romans 6.
